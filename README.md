# 🏎️ AUTOMOTIVE CULTURE

A modern automotive-themed website dedicated to the history, legacy, innovation, and motorsport heritage of Lamborghini.

---

## 📖 About The Project

AUTOMOTIVE CULTURE is a web-based project that explores Lamborghini's journey from its founding by Ferruccio Lamborghini in 1963 to its modern-day supercars and racing programs.

The website is designed to provide visitors with an engaging experience through:

- Lamborghini History
- Complete Lamborghini Model Lineup
- Motorsport Heritage
- FIA WEC Participation
- IMSA Racing Program
- GT3 Racing Program
- 24 Hours of Le Mans
- Interactive Quiz

---

## 🚗 Features

### 🏛️ Lamborghini History
Learn about the origins of Lamborghini, the vision of Ferruccio Lamborghini, and the milestones that shaped the brand.

### 🔥 All Lamborghini Series
Explore iconic Lamborghini models throughout history:

- 350 GT
- Miura
- Espada
- Countach
- Jalpa
- Diablo
- Murciélago
- Gallardo
- Reventón
- Aventador
- Huracán
- Urus
- Sián
- Revuelto

### 🏁 Motorsport Heritage
Discover Lamborghini's racing legacy, including:

- Lamborghini Super Trofeo
- Huracán GT3
- Temerario GT3
- SC63 LMDh Prototype
- FIA World Endurance Championship (WEC)
- IMSA SportsCar Championship
- FIA GT3 Competitions
- 24 Hours of Le Mans

### 🧠 Interactive Quiz
Test your knowledge about Lamborghini history, models, and motorsport achievements.
