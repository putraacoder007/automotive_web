The History of Lamborghini

1963

Ferruccio Lamborghini founded Automobili Lamborghini in Italy.

1966

Lamborghini Miura was introduced and became the world's first true supercar.

1974

Countach debuted with futuristic wedge styling and scissor doors.

1990

Diablo became Lamborghini's fastest production car.

2011

Aventador launched as Lamborghini's flagship V12 model.
