Lamborghini History

Lamborghini was founded in 1963 by Ferruccio Lamborghini in Sant'Agata Bolognese, Italy. The company was created to compete with Ferrari and quickly became famous for producing powerful V12 supercars with radical styling and innovative engineering.

Legendary Lamborghini Models

Lamborghini Miura

1966

Considered by many to be the world's first true supercar. The Miura introduced the mid-engine layout that would become standard for future exotic cars.

Lamborghini Countach

1974

Famous for its futuristic wedge shape and iconic scissor doors. A defining supercar of the 1970s and 1980s.

Lamborghini Diablo

1990

The first Lamborghini capable of exceeding 320 km/h and one of the most recognizable supercars of its era.

Lamborghini Murciélago

2001

The first V12 flagship developed during Lamborghini's Audi era, combining Italian passion with German engineering.

Lamborghini Aventador

2011

Featuring a carbon-fiber monocoque chassis and naturally aspirated V12 engine, the Aventador became a modern icon.

Lamborghini Motorsport Heritage

FIA WEC • IMSA • GT3 • 24 HOURS OF LE MANS

While Lamborghini is famous for producing some of the world's most exciting road cars, the brand has also built a growing motorsport legacy through Super Trofeo, GT3 racing, IMSA championships, endurance competitions, and the legendary 24 Hours of Le Mans.

2009

Lamborghini launches the Super Trofeo one-make championship.

2015

Huracán GT3 begins competing internationally.

2018

Multiple GT World Challenge victories across Europe and America.

2024

Lamborghini SC63 LMDh debuts at the 24 Hours of Le Mans.

Lamborghini Racing Legends

Lamborghini Super Trofeo

Since 2009, Super Trofeo has become Lamborghini's flagship one-make championship, featuring race-prepared Huracáns competing worldwide.

Lamborghini Huracán GT3

One of Lamborghini's most successful GT racing platforms, competing in GT World Challenge, IMSA, and endurance events.

Lamborghini Temerario GT3

Lamborghini's newest GT3 challenger, designed to continue the brand's success in international GT competition.

Lamborghini SC63 LMDh

Lamborghini's prototype for FIA World Endurance Championship and the legendary 24 Hours of Le Mans.
